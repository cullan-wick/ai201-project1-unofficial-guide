### About gBETA

gBETA runs multiple times every year in cities across the United States. Up to five companies from a range of verticals are selected from a competitive applicant pool to participate in the free seven-week program. Participating companies receive:

* Individualized coaching and mentorship from the team of nationally ranked accelerator gener8tor on company growth and investor readiness
* Weekly "Lunch & Learns" and "Mentor Swarms"
* Investor and accelerator pitches
* $1M+ in deals and perks from vendors like IBM Cloud, Rackspace, Amazon, Paypal, Zendesk, Hubspot and Microsoft
* Ongoing post-program support and gener8tor community involvement and offerings

gBETA's goal is that graduates will go on to participate in an investment accelerator or raise a seed round. You can see gBETA's progress against these goals here.

### Eligibility Criteria for the gBETA Frontier Technology Accelerator

### Based in Wisconsin

The gBETA Frontier Technology Accelerator is an early-stage program for high-growth startups with roots in Wisconsin, working in frontier technology sectors including advanced materials, AI, robotics, energy storage, and quantum computing.

![](https://7561636.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/7561636/gBETA%20Icons/gBETA%2BCommitment%2BIcon.webp)

### Program Commitment

You can commit to the full seven-week accelerator program, including biweekly coaching sessions, weekly Lunch & Learns, a Mentor Swarm, and an Investor Swarm in the final week. Active, consistent participation is essential to getting the most from the program.

![](https://7561636.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/7561636/gBETA%20Icons/gBETA%2BAccomplishment%2BIcon.webp)

### Frontier Technology Focus

Your startup is commercializing technology in one of our target sectors: advanced materials, advanced sensing, artificial intelligence, energy storage, intelligent systems, robotics, or quantum computing — and you're ready to pursue customers, partners, and capital.

![](https://7561636.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/7561636/gBETA%20Icons/gBETA_Light%2BBulb_S.webp)

### Coachability and Openness

You're coachable, open to feedback from mentors and investors, and willing to discuss your business openly. You thrive in a fast-paced environment and are ready to refine your value proposition, grow customer traction, and pitch to a network of 25–30 investors.

### Getting Started

**Program Kick-Off:** A 1.5-day event featuring a team-building activity, insightful content to guide you through the rest of the program, and introductory 1:1 meetings with your gBETA Program Manager.

![Weeks 1–6 icon](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60e4724ea746166606f95abb/e25caa8e-475f-4e6c-9374-6b0db67429a5/gBETA_Comms_S.png?format=300w)

### Weeks 1–6

**Team Meetings:** Two 1-hour strategy meetings each week with your founding team and Program Manager.

**Lunch & Learns:** Weekly 1-hour sessions on: Market and Competition, Revenue Models, Financing and Milestones, Comparables, Pitching, How to Get into an Accelerator, and more!

**Mentor Swarms:** One 2-hour “speed dating” session each week with a group of new mentors, for a total of ~30 mentor introductions over six weeks.

**Showcase:** Final celebration event with live pitches and networking with mentors, investors, and customers.

### gBETA Accelerator FAQs

Do you still have questions about the gBETA program? Explore some of our frequently asked questions below to find the answer.

* What is gBETA?

  gBETA is a free, seven-week, pre-accelerator program for companies that are looking to achieve rapid growth and/or raise venture capital. gBETA helps entrepreneurs work *in* and *on* their business while also attaining the entrepreneurial mindset needed to be successful.
* How much does it cost?

  Thanks to our program partners, gBETA is at no cost to the five companies selected to participate in each cohort.
* How is gBETA different from other accelerators?

  gBETA embraces a concierge approach to its programming. That means founders receive one-on-one attention throughout the program. Everything we do is personalized to your business, no matter how far along you are in its development.

  gBETA is run by nationally ranked startup accelerator gener8tor, so you know that the help you’re getting is top-notch.
* Should I apply to gBETA?

  Sure, what have you got to lose? It’s completely free, and we’ll help you grow and scale your business!

  gBETA is a good fit for you if you are ready to “step on the gas” for your business. We work with companies who are pre-revenue, and those who already have revenue. Most of our programs are industry-agnostic, so we accept a wide variety of startups, but we are looking for companies who want to scale quickly and see value in raising venture capital to do so.
* How will this help my business? What are the benefits?

  Through one-on-one interaction with their program manager, mentors and investors, program participants gain valuable personalized insights that can be applied to their business strategy. Program focus areas include gaining an entrepreneurial mindset, validating the company, growing customer traction, and preparing for investor pitches.

  Benefits include:

  • Additional media attention, which leads to additional support from community and business leaders

  • Access to gener8tor’s robust national network of thousands of fellow entrepreneurs, mentors and investors, and warm introductions to those most relevant to your business

  • Lifetime membership access to Morrow powered by GAN (Global Accelerator Network)

  • $1M + in deals and perks from vendors like Google, IBM Cloud, Rackspace, Amazon, PayPal, Zendesk and Microsoft

  • Membership in gener8tor’s startup community of thousands of startup founders, with access to gener8tor’s network, events and resources for the lifetime of your business
* What does the application/interview process look like?

  To be considered for the program, simply complete the online application. You can apply for multiple programs if you meet the eligibility criteria (e.g., presence in the relevant city or industry). After applications close, the director will notify you if you’re selected for an interview, which typically involves one or two rounds and a prepared pitch. Once interviews are complete, you'll be informed if you’ve been chosen.

  We also encourage you to sign up for office hours before or after applying to meet with the director and ask any questions.
* Is there follow-up assistance after gBETA?

  Once founders complete the 7-week program, they become alumni. Program Managers keep in close contact and continue to support their alumni, and alumni are part of the gener8tor network for the life of their business. We make every effort to ensure they are considered for future opportunities such as gener8tor’s investment accelerators, inclusion in our gener8tor conferences, and lightning rounds or introductions to new mentors and investors. We have your back from start to finish.
* What kind of time commitment will gBETA need from me? Do I need to be working on my startup full time?

  Running a startup and going through an accelerator is a commitment, but you don’t need to be working on your company full-time to participate in gBETA. We typically require 4-6 hours per week for meetings, plus additional time for follow-ups and for time that the founder needs to spend on their businesses. .

  Our goal is to focus on projects that will significantly advance your company, making it time well spent. We understand the balance between personal and work life, so our program managers will work with you to fit the program into your schedule. However, we do expect founders to make every effort to maximize their experience.
* Can I apply to gBETA more than once?

  Yes, please do! Due to the nature of the program and our desire to put a strong focus on five companies per cohort, not all who apply will be immediately accepted. Founders should continue to apply for gBETA even if they are not accepted upon first application.
* Will gBETA help me raise money for my business?

  Yes and no. We will help you set and achieve metrics that make your company attractive to investors and we’ll help you craft a killer pitch. We’ll also introduce you to investors both during the program and after the program, if you’d like us to. However, we won’t pitch or make asks for you. We’ll show you the path, but it’s up to you to make the journey and get the funding.

  While we cannot make guarantees in terms of potential investments, we work very hard to ensure that the program puts you in the best position possible to raise money if that is your goal.
* Which gBETA program should I apply to?

  Every gBETA program is different depending on the market for which you are applying. Some programs accept any type of startup that is located in a certain geography, while others may recruit nationally for a specific industry vertical.

  If you are unsure whether or not to apply, we highly recommend requesting office hours with the Program Manager of the program that interests you.
* Do I need to sign a Participation Agreement if I am selected to participate in gBETA?

  Although participation in gBETA is free, all participants are required to sign a participation agreement during the onboarding process.
* Should I be concerned about the protection of my intellectual property if I decide to apply?

  We treat all application data as confidential and don’t steal ideas. With thousands of applications yearly, many with similar concepts, we don’t sign NDAs. Our application doesn’t require your “special formula,” so only share what you're comfortable with
* I know a great company that should go through gBETA. How can I let gener8tor know?

  We’re eager to hear about companies you think would be a perfect fit for gBETA. **Click here** to share your referrals with us.
